Return & Refund Policy

We stand behind every product we sell. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward 30-day return policy.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at info@revengeworks.com or call (618) 207-3806 within 30 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  3. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.
  4. Once we receive the returned product, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days.
