Our Story

Revenge Works was born out of frustration. Like millions of pet owners, our founders loved their furry companions but were tired of products that only masked odors instead of eliminating them. After spending hundreds of dollars on cleaners that simply didn't work, we decided to create something better.

Working with biochemists and cleaning industry experts, we developed a professional-strength enzymatic formula that actually destroys odor-causing molecules at their source. No masking agents, no artificial fragrances covering up the problem—just pure enzymatic cleaning power that works.
