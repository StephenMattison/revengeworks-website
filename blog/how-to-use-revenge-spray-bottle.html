How to Use REVENGE! 32oz Spray for Best Results

Get the most out of your REVENGE! spray bottle with our step-by-step guide. Tips and tricks from 35+ years of professional carpet cleaning.

What's in the Bottle

REVENGE! Professional Strength Pet Odor & Stain Remover is a scientifically developed, environmentally safe water-based cleaner. It contains purified water, cleaners, and odor eliminators — no harsh chemicals, no ammonia, no bleach. It's ready to use right out of the bottle, no mixing or diluting required.

Surface-by-Surface Guide

Carpet & Rugs

  1. 1
    Blot first — For fresh stains, blot up excess moisture with clean white towels. Press firmly but don't rub.
  2. 2
    Spray generously — Apply REVENGE! to fully saturate the stained area. For old or deep stains, use enough product to penetrate through the carpet to the pad.
  3. 3
    Wait 10–15 minutes — Let the formula work. The active ingredients need time to break down odor-causing compounds at the molecular level.
  4. 4
    Blot dry — Use clean towels to absorb moisture. Repeat application for severe stains.
  5. 5
    Air dry — Allow to dry completely. A fan can speed the process. The odor is permanently eliminated once dry.

Upholstery & Mattresses

Use the same process as carpet, but be mindful of saturation levels. For couch cushions, spray both sides if possible. For mattresses, apply to the stained area and place clean towels underneath to absorb excess moisture. Allow extra drying time for thicker materials.

Hard Floors (Tile, Hardwood, Laminate)

Spray directly onto the stain and let sit for 5–10 minutes. Wipe clean with a damp cloth. For tile grout, where urine can seep into porous grouting, apply generously and let it work for the full 15 minutes before wiping. REVENGE! is safe for all water-safe surfaces.

Concrete (Garages, Basements, Patios)

Concrete is highly porous and absorbs urine deeply. Apply REVENGE! liberally and let it soak in for 15–20 minutes. For severe contamination, multiple applications may be needed. This is where the gallon size really shines — you'll need enough product to match the depth of penetration.

Pro Tips for Maximum Effectiveness

  • Act fast — Fresh stains are always easier to treat than old ones. Keep a bottle in every room where your pet spends time.
  • Use a blacklight — UV blacklights reveal old urine spots that are invisible to the naked eye. Treat every spot you find for complete odor elimination.
  • Don't skip the wait time — The formula needs time to work. Rushing the process means you'll likely need to reapply.
  • Refill from the gallon — The 32oz trigger sprayer is perfect for daily use. Refill it from the gallon jug for maximum value — you'll save over 50% per ounce.

When to Use the Gallon Jug Instead

The gallon jug is ideal for large-area treatments (whole rooms, large concrete areas), refilling your spray bottles, multi-pet households that go through product quickly, and professional cleaning jobs. Pour directly from the jug for large areas, or use it to refill your 32oz sprayer as needed.
