The Ultimate Guide to Eliminating Pet Odors from Your Home

A room-by-room breakdown of where pet odors hide and proven strategies to eliminate them permanently — not just cover them up.

If you're a pet owner, you've likely become nose-blind to odors that visitors notice the moment they walk through your door. Pet odors don't just live on the surface — they embed themselves deep into your home in places you might not expect. This guide will help you find them and eliminate them for good.

The Living Room: Furniture, Carpet & Hidden Spots

Your living room is ground zero for pet odors because it's where your pets spend most of their time. Odors accumulate in carpet fibers, area rugs, upholstered furniture, and throw pillows. But the hidden culprits are often behind and under furniture, where pet hair and dander accumulate in carpet fibers that never get vacuumed.

Action plan: Pull furniture away from walls and treat the carpet underneath with REVENGE! Use a blacklight to spot any hidden urine stains. Spray upholstery cushions (remove covers and spray both sides if possible).

The Bedroom: Mattresses & Bedding

If your pet sleeps with you (or sneaks onto the bed when you're away), your mattress is quietly absorbing oils, dander, and possibly urine. Mattresses are thick and porous, making them one of the toughest odor sources to treat. Pillows and comforters also trap pet odors over time.

Action plan: Strip the bed completely and spray the mattress surface with REVENGE! Place towels underneath to catch excess moisture. Allow to fully dry before remaking the bed. Wash bedding in hot water monthly.

The Basement & Garage: Concrete Floors

Concrete is one of the most challenging surfaces for pet odors because it's extremely porous. Urine soaks deep into unfinished concrete and can persist for years. If your pet has ever had accidents in the basement or garage, you likely need a thorough treatment.

Action plan: Pour REVENGE! directly onto the concrete from the gallon jug. Allow it to soak for 15–20 minutes. For severe contamination in larger areas, use a mop to spread the product evenly. Multiple applications may be necessary for deeply saturated concrete.

The Laundry Room: Pet Bedding & Towels

Pet beds, blankets, and the towels you use to dry your pets all accumulate odors over time. Regular washing helps, but sometimes the odor is embedded in the fabric and survives the wash cycle.

Action plan: Pre-treat pet bedding by spraying with REVENGE! before washing. For items that can't be machine-washed, spray thoroughly and allow to air dry. Replace pet beds that are beyond treatment.

The Entryway & Mudroom: Where Odors First Enter

Wet dog smell, muddy paws, and the general "outdoor" scent your pets bring inside all concentrate in your entryway. Entry mats, shoe storage areas, and coat hooks near the door are common odor hot spots.

Action plan: Treat entry mats and rugs regularly with REVENGE! Keep a 32oz spray bottle by the door for quick paw clean-ups and immediate accident response.

The Secret Weapon: A Systematic Approach

The reason most pet owners struggle with odors isn't that they're not cleaning — it's that they're only treating the spots they can see. Pet odors accumulate gradually in places you don't think to check. The most effective approach is a room-by-room deep treatment:

  1. Use a UV blacklight to scan every room (do this at night with the lights off for best results).
  2. Mark every spot you find with painter's tape so you can find them with the lights on.
  3. Treat systematically, one room at a time, using REVENGE! on every identified spot plus general areas like carpet edges and under furniture.
  4. Allow full drying before moving to the next room.
  5. Maintain by treating new accidents immediately and doing a blacklight scan quarterly.

Prevention Is Easier Than Remediation

Once you've done a thorough whole-house treatment, keeping things fresh is much easier. Keep a 32oz REVENGE! spray bottle in every area of your home where your pets spend time. Treat accidents immediately — fresh stains are dramatically easier to eliminate than set-in ones. And remember: with REVENGE!, you're not masking odors. You're destroying them permanently at the molecular level.
